ClubHub Feature Roadmap

We're presenting at the 2025 Engineering Expo, but it doesn't end there. Follow along with our journey in the coming years!

Forms and Affiliations

We're adding full support for Forms and Affiliations to feature match and serve as an alternative to Ideal-Logic. Forms are an important way for clubs to manage new members, secure funding and stay in complience with their Affiliations. Affiliations are an important way to associate your club with an organization or school. For example, clubs here are associated with OSU.

Synchronous Updates, Notifications, and Emails

For more responsive collaboration, we're integrating synchronous, realtime updates, notifications, and emails.

Integrations with Common Tools

We're adding Integrations for commonly used tools such as Discord, Zoom, Microsoft and Google suites. Find your club events on your Discord server, schedule zoom meetings automatically, and populate your calendar.

Finance Tracking and Cloud Storage Management

Supercharge your club or organization with new tools for tracking your financing, plus an easy way to manage and share documents or files on the cloud.

Search & Keyboard Shortcuts

Why not make ClubHub your own? We're adding custom key bindings for searching and navigating around your home for clubs and events!
